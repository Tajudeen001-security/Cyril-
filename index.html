Gbadamosi Tajudeen Olajide

Founder — JagX & JRILICENSE

Web developer and AI automator building social products under the JagX and JRILICENSE names.

The story

Most builders start with a tool. Gbadamosi started with a feed — the idea that a social app built by one person, for one community, could feel more alive than the giants it was competing with. That instinct became JagX Connect: posts, reels, live video, and chat, stitched together with JagX Coin, a gifting layer that gives the whole thing a pulse of its own economy.

It's the kind of project most teams would need a dozen engineers to ship — here it's one founder's name on the commits. That's the throughline across JRILICENSE and JagX: fast iteration, a product-first eye, and a willingness to build the full stack, from the interface down to the automation running quietly underneath it.

David Cyril

Founder — David Cyril Tech

Builder of APIs, AI tools, and everyday utilities out of Abuja, Nigeria.

The story

Where some builders chase one big flagship product, David Cyril Tech takes the opposite bet: ship a lot of small, useful things and let developers decide what sticks. That philosophy shows up in David Cyril APIs — a growing collection of RESTful endpoints other developers can plug straight into their own projects, no friction, no guesswork.

Nineteen public repositories and a following of 306 on GitHub later, the pattern is clear — downloaders, AI-powered tools, and the kind of small utilities that quietly become part of someone else's stack. It's infrastructure work, done in public, one repo at a time, out of Abuja.
